2024 Prospective

(I had to look up the opposite of "retrospective".)

I like CGP Grey and Myke Hurley's "Theme System", and I believe that discussing it in a public forum like this will help me pursue my chosen theme more effectively.

For 2024, the theme is intentionality. Among other things, this means:

  • Asking myself whether I enjoy doing something, or if habits and external factors have unduly driven me to do something self-destructive or wasteful.
  • Finding purposes for my actions and keeping them at the forefront of my mind, instead of doing things because I am supposed to.
  • Making realistic plans of action that are robust to schedule changes and the like.
  • Making time to work towards my long-standing goals, for which the plans of action are largely clear and stable.
  • Giving more attention to whatever I am doing, by choosing a singular focus and removing sources of distraction.

As of this writing, I won't publish concrete goals (resolution-style), and in theory those goals don't even exist as such. At the end of the year, I'll write up a retrospective about how I pursued this theme.
