A hopefully non-controversial list of things I do not like

These specific activities are not only themselves unpleasant, but the descriptions hopefully give a broader sense of how I think.

  • Programming language wars are exhausting and astonishingly unproductive. I would be happy if I didn't waste another moment discussing this with someone who was unwilling to put in the effort to learn, or unaware of the scope of their own ignorance.

    Peter Norvig and Oz Nova have been highly influential on my thoughts on learning programming.

  • Replacing my own writing with a large language model. Especially in English, the language in which I can best articulate myself, I choose my words carefully. Even if an LLM could write a "better" version, the words wouldn't be mine.
